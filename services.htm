 Urban Dog Walker

My services are:

  • Group walks - £13 per hour (£24 for two) - I walk social and friendly groups providing a high quality caring dog walking service. I will collect your dog and they will receive a full, fun 1 hour walk in a small group. I have 2 walks per day, 1 in the morning and 1 around lunch time.
     
  • Solo walks - £14.50 per hour - I offer a one hour solo walk for your dog. This can build up your dogs confidence or maybe they prefer to be walked alone. I also offer a half hour solo walk for the senior dog. AM & PM.
     
  • Full day care - £31 per day - I will pick up your dog and they will spend the day with me and my dogs. This includes 2 walks, then dropped home to you.
     
  • Puppy care - £10 for 30 minutes. Puppy visits for pups going through their inoculation period and when you cant be there. I will play with your pup, clean up any mess and give lots of cuddles! A visit lasts 30 minutes.
     
  • Cat care - £10 for 30 minutes. I will visit your cat in the morning, afternoon or evening; whichever you prefer or all 3! each visit includes feeding, water and litter tray cleaning. A good service for when you cant be home.
     
  • Small animal visit - £8 for 30 minutes for small animals that stay at home. They will be looked after while you are away on holiday or working. I can feed, water and spend time with your pet.
     
  • Pet taxi - From £10 - A taxi service for your pet. Off to the groomers or a vet appointment.

I am First Aid trained, police checked, fully insured and registered with Edinburgh Council.
