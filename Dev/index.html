Services

My services include:

Group walks

£13 per hour (£24 for two) - I walk social and friendly groups providing a high quality caring dog walking service. I will collect your dog and they will receive a full, fun 1 hour walk in a small group. I have 2 walks per day, 1 in the morning and 1 around lunch time.

Solo Walks

£15 per hour - I offer a one hour solo walk for your dog. This can build up your dogs confidence or maybe they prefer to be walked alone. I also offer a half hour solo walk for the senior dog. AM & PM.

Full day care

£31 per day - I will pick up your dog and they will spend the day with me and my dogs. This includes 2 walks, then dropped home to you.

Puppy care

£10 for 30 minutes. Puppy visits for pups going through their inoculation period and when you cant be there. I will play with your pup, clean up any mess and give lots of cuddles! A visit lasts 30 minutes.

Cat care

£10 for 30 minutes. I will visit your cat in the morning, afternoon or evening; whichever you prefer or all 3! each visit includes feeding, water and litter tray cleaning. A good service for when you cant be home.

Pet taxi

From £10 - A taxi service for your pet. Off to the groomers or a vet appointment.

About

  • About me

    My name is Zoë Gibson and I'm a professional dog walker. I have 3 dogs and it's because of them that I started Edinburgh Urban Dog Walker. I wanted to have a career where I could spend more time with them and combine my love of animals.

  • Why walk with me

    It is really important to me as a dog owner to know that any person looking after my dogs are caring and trustworthy so this is what I strive to provide for you and your pet. I have grown up with animals around me and have had many years of experience with them. I am First Aid trained, police checked, fully insured and registered with Edinburgh Council.
